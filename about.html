About Us

Quality You Can Trust

At Resistance Restoration & Remodeling LLC, we provide high-quality restoration and remodeling services with precision and dedication. Every project is handled with care and professionalism to ensure your property reaches its full potential.

Vision Statement

To become Southwest Florida’s most trusted name in restoration and remodeling — recognized for our integrity, craftsmanship, and client-centered approach. We aim to set the standard for reliable, budget-friendly services where one call truly solves it all.

Our Story

With over a decade of hands-on experience in drywall, remodeling, and general home improvement, Resistance Restoration & Remodeling LLC has built a solid reputation for quality, reliability, and transparency. Led by a licensed Florida Gypsum Drywall contractor, our team proudly serves Fort Myers, Sanibel Island, Pine Island, and surrounding areas — turning visions into reality, one project at a time.
